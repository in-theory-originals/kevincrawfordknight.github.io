Back in 2001, I entered the annual Writing Contest held by my local Hermosa Beach newspaper (the "Easy Reader"). I submitted this original poem:

"She stood on the balcony,
Inexplicably mimicking his hiccupping,
Amicably welcoming him in."

I didn't win any prize money. Which seems fair. But a couple decades later, I remembered it, and I Googled it. So many hits. Speech practice for actors, tongue-twisters, viral TikToks. Too funny!
