philipp koehn Former PhD student in Computer Science at USC/ISI My count of digital fame:

I was a PhD Student in Computer Science at the University of Southern California and a Research Assistant in the Natural Language Group at the Information Sciences Institute in 1997-2003. My advisor was Kevin Knight.
I am currently a Postdoc at MIT. See my web page there for more recent information.
